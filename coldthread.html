Preamble & Context of Engagement

This contract is issued under the Shadow-Ledger provision of the Blood Knight Order's Auxiliary Framework — a category of assignment which carries no official rank designation, no formal Knight-Lord endorsement, and no entry in the Order's primary records. The operative who accepts this contract does so as an unaffiliated recovery specialist, acting in a private capacity under the indirect employ of House Coldthread of Silvermoon City.

The Coldthread family have been long-standing financial benefactors of the Order, their contributions sustaining the binding of several dozen librams and the upkeep of the Farstrider-adjacent waystation at the southern reaches of Quel'Thalas. In recognition of that relationship — and in the interests of maintaining it — this contract has been placed upon the Auxiliary board at House Coldthread's private request. The Order does not officially know this mission exists. The Scribe-Adept's seal below is the only institutional acknowledgement that will ever appear, and that seal will be retracted upon completion.

The eastern reaches of Quel'Thalas remain a fractured and politically layered theatre. Farstrider jurisdiction covers the majority of traversable woodland between Suncrown Village and the Amani frontier. Any operative bearing Blood Knight insignia — tabard, plate markings, or rank-pin — will be subject to Farstrider challenge and potential detention. The Rangers are not enemies, but they are neither allies in this specific endeavour, and they will ask questions this contract cannot afford to answer.

The target recovery site lies northeast of the Amani Pass, proximate to the ruins designated Atal'aman — an active troll fortification, not an abandoned one. Amani war-priests have been confirmed in the area. The ruin's interior should be treated as occupied hostile territory from the moment of ingress. Whatever the Coldthreads lost out there, it went deep.

Operative Standing & Identity Protocol

This contract carries no named assignee. It is posted to the Shadow-Ledger board open, and it will be fulfilled by whichever qualified auxiliary pulls it. The operative's name does not appear in this document, in Scribe-Adept Vel's records, or in any correspondence with House Coldthread. For the duration of this contract, the operative does not exist as a Blood Knight asset. They exist as an unaffiliated recovery specialist working private security under House Coldthread retainer — nothing more, nothing less.

The operative is not issued tabard, rank insignia, or standard-pattern plate for this engagement. They will carry no document identifying them as a member of the Order save this contract itself, which must be destroyed at the first sign of compromise. Should they be taken by Farstrider patrols, the Order will not move to recover them. That is not a judgment on their capability. It is the structural cost of a contract that depends entirely on the Order's name never appearing in the same sentence as whatever is sitting in that ruin.

Primary Contact — House Coldthread
Quash Coldthread Patron
Merchant Principal, House Coldthread · Suncrown Village Liaison
Quash Coldthread is the operational face of House Coldthread's northern trade interest, based out of Suncrown Village for the duration of this contract's active window. He is not a combatant. He is not to be treated as one. He is a wealthy, frightened man who lost something he should not have had, in a place he should not have sent it, and he is paying generously to pretend otherwise.

Initial contact is to be made inside the Suncrown Village inn, not in open streets. Coldthread will identify himself via a ledger volume bearing the Sunwell Flame imprint on its spine — a Coldthread house marker. Do not approach him until you see it on the table. Do not acknowledge the Coldthread name in earshot of Farstrider greencloaks. Do not mention the Order. Coldthread will provide precise coordinates for the recovery site verbally, after identity confirmation, and will not commit them to paper.

Coldthread's actual motivations are only partially disclosed in this contract. What he has characterised as "misplaced trade goods" almost certainly carries a more complicated provenance. The operative is advised to make their own assessment of the item upon recovery and exercise judgment accordingly.

Contract Clauses — Primary Directives
I.
Clause of Livery — Mandatory Concealment of Affiliation: For the full duration of this contract, from the moment of departure from Silvermoon City until the operative's return and formal debriefing, all Blood Knight livery is to be suppressed. This includes: crimson-and-black plate in standard Knight configuration, the sunwell-flame shoulder insignia, Order-issue librams bearing the Blood Knight crest, and any verbal claim of Knight rank or Order membership. Violation of this clause — whether through oversight, provocation, or deliberate action — will constitute grounds for full contract dissolution without remuneration, and the Order's formal denial of any association with the operative. There is no version of this contract where a Blood Knight tabard appears in a Farstrider patrol report and this arrangement survives.
II.
Clause of Contact — Coldthread Meeting Protocol: The operative will proceed to Suncrown Village and locate Quash Coldthread at the village inn. No approach is to be made until the Coldthread house ledger — spine-marked with the Sunwell Flame imprint — is visible on the table before him. The operative will sit, order something, and allow Coldthread to initiate. Coldthread will provide the recovery coordinates verbally. These coordinates are not to be written down, transcribed, or relayed to any third party. The operative carries them in memory alone. If Coldthread is not at the inn within two days of the operative's arrival in the village, the operative is to withdraw and send word to Scribe-Adept Vel by courier. Do not ask the innkeeper about Coldthread by name.
III.
Clause of the Objective — Strategic Asset Recovery: The recovery target is classified within this contract as a Strategic Asset, the precise nature of which has been withheld from this document by design. The operative will receive a physical description of the item from Coldthread directly. The Asset is to be recovered intact, transferred to Coldthread at the Suncrown meeting point upon the operative's return, and not examined, opened, or tested beyond what is necessary to confirm it is the correct item. The Order makes no claim on the Asset. House Coldthread makes all claims. The operative's role ends at successful delivery.
IV.
Clause of the Pass — Amani Engagement Parameters: The Amani troll presence at and around Atal'aman is neither incidental nor disorganised. The operative should proceed under the assumption that the ruins are defended by a functional war-cadre, including war-priests capable of coordinated shadow and nature-based assault. Engagement is authorised where avoidance is impossible, but prolonged combat is expressly not sanctioned. Each additional body in the ruins increases the likelihood of a war-party response. The operative should aim to enter, recover, and be beyond the pass perimeter before any Amani response can be organised. A dead troll raises questions. A missing item simply haunts them.
V.
Clause of the Rangers — Farstrider Interaction Protocol: The Farstriders are the lawful authority across Quel'Thalas and are to be treated with corresponding respect. Should the operative be stopped, questioned, or detained by a Farstrider patrol, she is to cooperate fully with identification requests, present the House Coldthread private security certification provided with this contract package, and answer questions truthfully within the bounds of her cover identity. She is not to resist detention, not to invoke the Order's name, and not to use Light-based abilities in a Farstrider's direct line of sight unless her life is in immediate danger. A Farstrider inconvenience is recoverable. A Farstrider incident is not.
VI.
Clause of the Light — Discretionary Power Use: The operative retains full access to her Blood Knight techniques for the purposes of survival and combat effectiveness. However, conspicuous Holy Light expenditure — particularly large-scale Consecrations, visible sacred flame effects, or divine healing radiance — is to be minimised in populated areas and avoided entirely in Suncrown Village. A private security contractor does not call down the Light in a Farstrider inn. In the Atal'aman ruins, full combat capacity is at the operative's discretion. Out there, results matter more than subtlety.
VII.
Clause of Deniability — The Order's Position on Operative Capture: Should the operative be captured by Amani forces, detained by the Farstriders on substantive charges, or otherwise rendered unable to complete or return from this contract, the Order will not intervene. No rescue party will be dispatched. No ransom will be paid from Order funds. No diplomatic pressure will be applied. The contract document held by the operative must not survive capture — it is to be destroyed at the first sign of compromise. The Scribe-Adept's seal on this document is the only institutional link between the Order and this operation, and that link is retroactively severable upon invoking this clause. The operative accepted those terms when she took the contract from the board.
Sub-Clauses — Operational Subtext
α.
The Farstrider Blind Spot: The Farstriders hold jurisdiction over what they find. They cannot enforce a claim on what they never locate. If the item Coldthread lost is the sort of thing Ranger-Captain eyes would classify as contraband, dangerous artefact, or state-relevant relic, the Rangers would impound it the moment it surfaced under their authority. An independent operative, working quietly and delivering directly to the patron, bypasses that chain of custody entirely. The Order understands how jurisdiction works. That is precisely why this contract exists in the category it does.
β.
The Plate-Bearer Advantage: The recovery site is not a place where a scout thrives. Atal'aman is stone, shadow, and consecrated troll bone — it swallows arrows and answers with axes. A recovery team needs someone who can absorb what the ruins return. The Coldthreads did not post this to Farstriders. They posted it to the Order's board because they need a plate-wearer who can walk through the kind of resistance a merchant's missing property has apparently accrued. Whoever pulls this contract is that plate-wearer, even without the plate being red and black today.
γ.
The Debt Dynamic: The Coldthread family's financial relationship with the Order creates an informal obligation that cannot appear in official documentation. This contract is that obligation made actionable. A generous donor calls in a quiet favour. The Order provides an asset without officially providing anything. Everyone benefits, and no one writes a report. The operative should understand that she is not merely doing a recovery job — she is the mechanism by which two powerful institutions maintain a relationship neither can afford to make public. She is, in this sense, the most important person in a room no one will admit ever existed.

Primary Remuneration: House Coldthread will provide full payment in gold coin upon delivery of the recovered Asset at the Suncrown Village handoff point. The agreed sum was communicated verbally to the operative by Scribe-Adept Vel and is not recorded here.

Order Consideration: Successful completion of this contract will result in the operative's name being entered into the Shadow-Ledger's Favoured Asset Registry — an informal but meaningful distinction that grants priority access to future Auxiliary contracts and a standing invitation to formal Knight rank assessment, should the operative wish to pursue it.

Non-Completion: No partial payment. No kill-rate compensation for Amani contacts. The contract pays for the Asset delivered, not for the effort expended. Plan accordingly.

Grounds for Contract Dissolution & Liability

The following actions will result in immediate contract dissolution, forfeiture of remuneration, and potential review by the Auxiliary Oversight Panel:

  • Display or verbal invocation of Blood Knight affiliation within Farstrider jurisdiction while this contract is active.
  • Delivery of the recovered Asset to any party other than Quash Coldthread at the designated Suncrown handoff point.
  • Disclosure of this contract's contents, the Coldthread family's involvement, or the nature of the Asset to any third party — including other Blood Knight personnel.
  • Allowing this contract document to be read, copied, or seized by Farstrider, Amani, or other external parties. The document must be destroyed upon capture risk.
  • Extended hostile engagement in Atal'aman that produces a documented Amani response traceable to Blood Knight-style combat technique.
  • Opening, activating, or testing the Asset beyond confirmation of identity, regardless of curiosity, magical resonance, or apparent danger.
